Music Production.

Kai Hagen (b. 2002, Tsukuba, Japan) is a multi-disciplinary artist residing on the unceded territories of the Sylix Okanagan peoples.

His main interests are in sound, music, graphic design, film, photography, generative digital art and VR. He graduated in the spring of 2024 with a Bachelor of Media Studies degree at the University of British Columbia Okanagan with a minor in visual arts.

He has professional experience in music production, audio engineering, sound design, film, branding and marketing as well as experience exhibiting artworks in virtual reality, augmented reality, photography, and creative coding.

He has installed artworks both internationally at the University of Exeter through the AMP labâ€™s PressPlay! project of 2023 as well as locally in the FINA gallery at UBCO and the Rotary Centre for the Arts in downtown Kelowna. He has worked as an undergraduate research assistant with multiple university labs including the Okanagan Visualization and Interaction lab, ReMedia Lab, and the AMP lab.

He is also a music producer and audio engineer having worked together with Vot, an artist based out of Vancouver, to create a debut album titled Vot.
